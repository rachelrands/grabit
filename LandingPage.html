Main Purposes of Grabbit

No need for you to tag 100 people in your next group picture on social media. Simply upload your photo to grabbit, broadcast the password, and everyone can grab the photo from the cloud. You can set up location preferences based on the group of people you are sharing with. Grabbit even works for extremely large groups of crowds, so everyone can access it. After 30 minutes, your file will dissaprear. So you will never have to worry about the wrong people accessing your information on a later time. 

Have you ever taken a group photo of a bunch of people, but never knew them well enough to send them the picutre? Or have you ever been in a group project where you wanted to share a file, but didnt want to go through the hassle of adding all sorts of new contacts to your phone? Grabbit fixes these issues.

 

 Grabbit allows any user to upload any file for 30 minutes with whatever specified location the user prefers. The user will create a secret password for the file, and anyone with the password can access the file and download it in real time, without going through the long and tedious efforts of exchaning contact information. Grabbit is perfect for groups and simple to use.
